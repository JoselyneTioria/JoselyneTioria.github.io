About Me

Hello! My name is Joselyne Tioria. I am a 21-year-old design student currently studying at Zhejiang University in China. Originally from Indonesia, I bring a unique cultural perspective to my work and studies.

My MBTI is INTJ, and my zodiac sign is Virgo. These traits shape my approach to design, photography, and creative expression.

My Passion

I love capturing candid moments through photography. For me, a photograph is more than just an image—it's a story, a feeling, or a thought. Sometimes I describe what I felt when I took the picture; other times, I write poetry or share a deep thought (思考) to convey the essence of the moment.

"A photograph is a silent poem, a snapshot of thoughts frozen in time."

Interests

Music is a big part of my life. It inspires me and often sets the mood for my creative projects. Whether I'm editing photos or brainstorming ideas, you can always find me listening to my favorite tracks.
