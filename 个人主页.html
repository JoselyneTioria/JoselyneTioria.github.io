Hi, I'm Joselyne Tioria, a 21-year-old design student at Zhejiang University in China, originally from Indonesia. I love capturing candid moments through my photography and creating stories behind each shot. As an INTJ and a Virgo, I’m detail-oriented and enjoy exploring ideas deeply, whether it's through design projects or the meaning behind a photo. I’m fascinated by nature and how its patterns can be a source of inspiration. When I’m not busy with design or snapping photos, you’ll probably find me immersed in music, letting the rhythm spark new ideas.
I believe that every photograph has a story waiting to be told. My passion lies in capturing images that go beyond the visual, evoking emotions and sparking curiosity about the world and people around us. From landscapes to intimate portraits, each shot I take is infused with a narrative, reflecting moments of beauty, introspection, and human connection. Through my lens, I strive to create images that resonate deeply, inviting viewers to discover the layers of meaning within.
